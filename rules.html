General Room Rules for Trap City [8/5/14 Last Updated]

Things in Pink will be responded by muting
Things in Blue will be responded by a mod with a warning
Things in Red will be responded with a 1 hour ban
Things in Green will give you a permanent or 1 day ban depending which mods are there at the moment
  1. English only. If you don’t speak English after a few warnings, you will be muted.
  2. Avoiding mutes will get you banned. Depending on the moderator, you may get permanently banned.
  3. NSFW images, videos, music videos being played and gifs are not allowed. If you post a NSFW link 2 times you will be banned.
  4. No Auto-joining waitlist while being AFK. If we remove you from the waitlist and you autojoin back, you will be rewarded with a 1 hour/1 day ban.
  5. No Auto-Mehing, if you continue to do so you will be banned.
  6. Play songs that suit the room theme. If you continue to play songs that isn’t Trap, you will be banned.
  7. No troll songs, trolling & bullying. If you continue to do so you will be banned.
  8. No racial discrimination what so ever.
  9. Do not promo/advertise other rooms, if you continue to advertise you will be banned.
  10. Do not ask to be mod. When we’re ready for more mods, we’ll mod people. This includes Resident DJ.
  11. Do not ask for skips, if you continue you will be muted.
  12. Absolutely no spamming. If you continue to spam you will be banned. A ban will depend on what you spam.
  13. Respect all staff members. If you continue to insult a mod they WILL ban you. Ban will vary with mod.

DJ Rules/Guidelines [8/5/14 Last Updated]

  1. We only allow EDM Trap and the sub-genres of trap (such as trapstyle, Festival, neuro trap, chill-trap, future bass/beats, Jersey Club, dirty south, trill trap)!
  2. 7 minute max song length
  3. 1hr AFK limit
  4. You may refresh your page while on booth.
  5. Do not change the song title from the original (just don’t remove the artist’s credit).
  6. Do not play a song that has been already played in the history list.
  7. Always review the history to make sure the song you are about to play hasn’t been played already. Please contact mod if you’re unsure about your song choice.
  8. You will be skipped once you played a song that is still in the history and the bot will automatically add you to 2nd place in the waitlist.
  9. Please review overplayed song list here so you won’t get skipped.
